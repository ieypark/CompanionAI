Voice-first companionship for seniors

Aging with connection,

CompanionAI is a voice-first companion designed to reduce loneliness and support emotional wellbeing through natural, respectful conversation. Simple to use, built for aging, and focused on human connection.

Request information See features Business model

CompanionAI is a companionship and engagement platform (not a clinical device, and not a monitoring tool).

What it does

Features are designed to deepen connection and encourage gentle engagement in daily life.

Natural conversation

Voice-first conversations tailored to personal history and preferences.

Reminiscence support

Gentle prompts that encourage storytelling and cognitive engagement.

Family messages

Secure delivery of family updates in a warm, familiar format.

Friendly wellness prompts

Supportive suggestions that feel like care, not alarms.

Positioning: CompanionAI supports companionship and engagement. It does not diagnose, treat, monitor, or provide emergency response.

Approach

Warm, respectful, quietly empowering. Designed for comfort and dignity.

CompanionAI adapts to each person’s routine and story, stays patient through repetition, and supports autonomy. The goal is a consistent daily presence that complements families and care staff.

  • Voice-first accessibility for low digital literacy
  • Dementia-aware interaction style (calm and patient)
  • Human-centered delivery (no tech-heavy experience)

Business model

Affordable access for individuals, with institutional pricing for care settings.

CompanionAI is positioned as a companionship and engagement platform supporting emotional wellbeing. It does not make medical claims and is designed to complement families and care staff.

Non-clinical Non-monitoring Dignity-first design Voice-first accessibility

Impact

A practical path toward less loneliness and stronger everyday connection.

We aim to help seniors feel valued rather than invisible, and help families feel connected rather than helpless. Success is measured through reduced loneliness, increased cognitive engagement through conversation, and restored family connection.

We also believe empathetic aging technology can become an area of Canadian leadership globally, grounded in trust and accessibility.
